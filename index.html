About Us

Volt Fitness gym in 6th October City is a unique experience of training and body shaping. Our mission is to provide a healthy modern atmosphere for fitness workout and strength training, with special emphasis on trainee guidance and support. All body muscle machines and equipment are scientifically approved and safe. Trainers and coaches are ISSA and NASM certified. We provide classes and private sessions for ladies, and our personal training services are truly unique. Membership packages are scalable and flexible to accommodate all your needs. The gym always maintains clean floors, fresh air, and positive energy, with no overcrowding or sound harassment. With a high benefit-to-cost ratio, Volt Fitness guarantees customer satisfaction as our top priority.

3rd District Gym

The 3rd District branch stands out with a professional CrossFit hall, fully equipped with all CrossFit training essentials including free racks, multiple pull-up bars, kettlebells, medicine balls, plyo boxes, ropes, and more. CrossFit is highly popular for its ability to improve both fitness and strength, while creating a unique atmosphere of competition and challenge. Practicing CrossFit requires attending classes and personal training sessions to ensure safe performance and maximize results. This branch also features a free weights hall, a hall with essential strength machines, and modern cardio equipment.
